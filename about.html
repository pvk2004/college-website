CVR COLLEGE OF ENGINEERING

The CVR College of Engineering was established in 2000. It is approved by the All India Council for Technical Education and accredited by the National Board of Accreditation, India. CVR College of Engineering was affiliated with Jawaharlal Nehru Technological University, Hyderabad. The college is located in Mangalpally(V), Ibrahimpatnam(M), Ranga Reddy, 20 km from the center of Hyderabad, India. The college is supported by the Cherabuddi Educational Society.

INFRASTRUCTURE

Spread over a built-up area of over 36000 square metres on a 32-acre (130,000 m2) campus, the college has 71 classrooms, 23 professors' rooms, 60 teachers' rooms, six air-conditioned conference rooms, 40 labs, and about 1280 computer systems, supported by UPS and continuous power back-up from generators.
There are 1186 computers in the departments. The college has an ELCS (English Learning and Communication Skills) lab.
There is a three-story building for the exclusive use of library and postgraduate research facilities.
The CVR library has 35,000 books and 280+ journals (including IEEE Journals). The auditorium, seating 1500 people, is used for workshops, conferences and seminars organized by the college and is fully air-conditioned.
A fleet of around 57 buses serve the college from all parts of the city. The canteen provides food to students and staff.

ACTIVITIES

CVR College of Engineering organizes an annual technical festival, Ciencia.

CVR College of Engineering organizes state level inter college annual sports meet, ENGINEERING PREMIER LEAGUE -EPL(started in 2017).
